About Us

Hey Viewer! This is Arshad, a graduate student and a developer. I have interests in non-fiction books and the stories behind their authors. This passion led me to research famous non-fiction books globally and compile them in one place. I utilized HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build this website from scratch.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive collection of non-fiction books that educate, inform, and inspire readers around the world. We aim to make these resources accessible to everyone, promoting lifelong learning and curiosity.

Our Vision

We envision a world where knowledge is freely accessible, and everyone has the opportunity to learn from the greatest minds in history. By curating a diverse range of non-fiction books, we hope to contribute to a more informed and enlightened society.
